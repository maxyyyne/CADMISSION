About the College of Computer Studies

Welcome to the College of Computer Studies, where we offer comprehensive programs designed to prepare students for the ever-evolving field of technology. Our programs aim to provide students with the theoretical knowledge and practical skills needed to excel in various technology-related careers.The College of Computer Studies offers a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS), a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT), and a Masters in Information Technology (MSIT). Each program is designed to cater to different interests and career goals, ensuring that our students receive the best education tailored to their aspirations.

With a focus on hands-on learning and real-world experience, our students have the opportunity to work on projects that solve real-world problems, gaining valuable experience and making a positive impact even before they graduate. Our faculty are industry experts who bring their knowledge and experience into the classroom, providing students with insights into the latest trends and technologies.Join us at the College of Computer Studies and take the first step towards a successful career in technology. We are committed to helping our students achieve their goals and make a difference in the world of technology.
